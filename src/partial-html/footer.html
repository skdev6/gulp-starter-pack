About Abacus Self Storage

Since 1999, Abacus has provided top-tier self-storage, earning thousands of satisfied, repeat customers who frequently refer us. We remain committed to excellence in security, cleanliness, maintenance, and friendly service.

To achieve this, we continually enhance our services and facilities. Key improvements include a natural gas generator, extensive security cameras, motion sensor lighting, a new steel roof, LED lights, an upgraded entrance gate, a new HVAC system, and a solar power system installed in 2020. Contact us to learn more about our commitment to excellence.
